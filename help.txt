coindust v0.0.1
usage: coindust <command> [<args>]

$ coindust new
creates new bitcoin address and private key

$ coindust balance <bitcoin address>|--wallet <filename>
check the balance of an address or an entire wallet

$ coindust tx --in <address> --out <address> --amount <btc> --wallet <filename>
builds bitcoin transaction. Safe to use - DOES NOT SEND.
Use https://blockchain.info/pushtx to submit transaction.
supported wallet format: dump file from bitcoin-core
